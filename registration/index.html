REGISTRATION

● Registration fees can be paid only after the acceptance of the paper. At least one author of each accepted paper must register at full fee on or before March 3, 2023.

● The Registration fee includes admission to all keynote lectures, scientific sessions, conference E-proceedings and E-certificates for the registered author only.

● The Registration fee is non-refundable.
